The GT100 classic car hire and driving club

A member’s car club where you can hire and drive a range of classic cars from our nostalgic based collection. If you love cars such as Maserati, Porsche, BMW and Mercedes from the fabulous era of the 80s and 90s, then The GT100 Club is the place for you.

About the club

You choose your preferred annual membership from 3 tiers in exchange for an agreed amount of club points. You can then use your points to hire out any of our cars whenever you desire and hassle free all year round.

We also arrange and host various club driving experience days throughout the year.

Open to both members and non-members, our driving days give you a chance to enjoy driving 5 different cars on some of the best Kent roads and beyond.

From only £250 per person, they make the ideal birthday present or special gift for you, your family or friends. Alternatively you can talk to us about booking one of our days exclusively for your group or corporate days out.
