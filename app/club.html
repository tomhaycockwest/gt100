Welcome to The GT100 Club

An exclusive members only car hire and driving club. A place for people to enjoy driving a stunning collection of nostalgic 80s and 90s classics.

With an expanding garage of 25 interesting, memorable and often rare cars, we have cars for all occasions. For that day out with the family, trip away with friends, or those moments to yourself, our cars will take you and your passengers back down memory lane, bringing you smiles all the way.

Sharing our cars to give you fun and hassle free driving is our number 1 priority. Availability, reliability and condition are what we promise our members. Of course we are always looking to add cars to our collection and as members we hope you will help us choose along the way!
