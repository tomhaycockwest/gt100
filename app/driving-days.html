Driving experience days

Join The GT100 Club on one of hosted days out and experience driving different cars from our range of memorable 80s and 90s classics.

Open to both members and non-members, we give you the opportunity to join up to 10 people driving 5 different cars on our various themed driving experience days.

We have searched out some of the best and most scenic driving roads in Kent for you to enjoy, and don’t worry we won’t all just be driving in a convoy all day as there will be plenty of competition and surprises for you to enjoy along the way!

With prices starting from only £250 per person and everything included from petrol and insurance to lunch at some lovely Kent pubs, we make sure you have a memorable, fun and special day.

So for that ideal birthday present or special gift, a planned day out for you and your friends and family, or even just an excuse to spoil yourself, why not come along and try a driving day with the GT100 Club. Drivers must be over 28 and have no more than 6 points.

See our available days

By seeing the cars we are going to include in advance of each of our driving days means you can choose the day that best suits you. We hold days out on both weekdays and weekends to make it easier for everyone to enjoy.

See our cars

You can also take a day exclusively for your group. Tailor your day, from your car selection to the routes you take or extra activities along the way.

So if you have a requirement to arrange that special social, corporate or business day out then please talk to us about how we can help tailor you something extra special for you and your guests.
