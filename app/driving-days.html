Memorable driving days

If you looking for a great and different way to entertain your
team or customers, then let The GT100 Club and our collection of
cars from the 80s and 90s create that special and memorable day.

Find Out More

Choose up to 5 cars and 10 guests, and everyone gets to drive each car during their experience. You can tailor your day, from your car selection to the routes you take or extra activities along the way.

To ensure your day remains special, a club host and mechanic will join you in a supporting vehicle, leaving everyone to enjoy the experience, thrills and smiles that 80s and 90s classics will bring you.

Our Cars

We will also arrange events for our members to try out multiple cars in one day, meet new people and gain new experiences.

So for that drive through the great Kent countryside or down to the beach for fish and chips, let The GT100 Club take you away for some really memorable and smile making journeys.
